VOCALOIDS

Vocaloids are singing voice sythesizers, often portrayed in a body drawn in an anime style,that are used to sing songs and covers. They're produced by various companies such as YAMAHA, Crypton Future Media, Inc, etcetera. Videos are usually posted on Nico Nico Douga, a Japanese site somewhat like youtube.
